What's in the Box

Every month brings a new lineup of fictional-yet-totally-believable indie favorites. Here's a taste of what subscribers rave about.

🇯🇵 Japan

Hokkaido Sea Salt Matcha Crunch

Crispy rice senbei glazed with authentic ceremonial-grade matcha and finished with a pinch of sea salt. It’s earthy, slightly bittersweet, and the perfect balance of sweet and savory.

🇰🇷 Korea

Spicy Ghost Pepper Rice Cakes

Puffed tteokbokki rice chips dusted in sweet gochugaru and real ghost pepper. It starts out sweet, but a slow-building heat kicks in that will have you reaching for more.

🇹🇼 Taiwan

Sun Moon Lake Pineapple Cloud Bites

Traditional, buttery shortcrust pastry pockets packed with a rich, chewy, caramelized golden pineapple filling. A classic Taiwanese night-market comfort treat that melts in your mouth.

🇹🇭 Thailand

Bangkok Chili-Mango Leather Strips

Tangy, chewy dried mango ribbons tossed in spicy prik kee noo chili and a squeeze of fresh lime. It’s sweet, intensely sour, and packing serious heat in every single pull.

🇭🇰 Hong Kong

Mong Kok Egg Waffle Crisps

Your favorite street-market gai daan jai reimagined into thin, crispy snack wafers. They have that signature rich, sweet egg custard aroma with a warm dash of vanilla.

🇯🇵 Japan

Kyoto Kinako Mochi Chocolate Cubes

Chewy, pillowy mochi bites covered in rich dark chocolate and generously dusted with toasted soybean powder. Deeply nutty, sweet, and incredibly satisfying to chew.
